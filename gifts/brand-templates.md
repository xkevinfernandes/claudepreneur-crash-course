# Brand File Templates

> Blank templates for brand.md, offer.md, and icp.md.
> Fill these in for any business — yours, a client's, a project.

---

## brand.md — How You Sound

```markdown
# Brand Voice

> How you sound. Read by ALL content skills.

## Voice
[Describe your communication style. Are you casual and direct? Professional and warm? Blunt and funny? Give examples of how you'd actually talk to a client.]

## Signature Phrases
[Any phrases, ideas, or concepts you come back to again and again. Things your clients hear from you regularly.]

## Language Rules
- **Banned words/phrases:** [What makes you cringe? What do you never want to sound like?]
- **Tone:** [Your energy level — intense? Calm? Encouraging? No-nonsense?]
- **Style:** [Short sentences? Long and flowing? Metaphors? Stories? Bullet points?]

## What I DON'T Sound Like
[Describe what you actively avoid. A specific person, brand, or type of content that's the opposite of you.]
```

## offer.md — What You Sell

```markdown
# Offer

> Your main product/service. Read by CTA and sales-facing skills.

## What I Sell
[Your main offer. Coaching, consulting, a course, a service, a product. Be specific — what does someone actually get?]

## The Transformation
- **Before:** [Where your client is when they start. Their situation, frustrations, what's not working.]
- **After:** [Where they are when you're done. Results, feelings, changes.]

## Price Point
[Approximate range — $50, $500, $5,000. This helps Claude calibrate CTAs and language.]

## CTA
[What's the natural next step for an interested person? Book a call? Join a community? Download something? Buy now?]
```

## icp.md — Your Dream Client

```markdown
# Ideal Client Profile

> Your dream customer. Read by ALL audience-facing skills.

## Who They Are
[Describe them as a person, not a demographic. What's their day like? What stage are they at? What kind of work do they do?]

## Their Pains
[Top 3 frustrations. What keeps them up at night? What have they already tried that didn't work?]

## Their Desires
[What do they actually want? Not what they say — what they'd pay anything for.]

## Their Objections
[What stops them from buying? What do they tell themselves at 2am? "It's too expensive" is surface — go deeper.]

## Their Language
[The exact words and phrases THEY use to describe their problem. Not your words — theirs. What would they search for on Google at midnight?]
```

## CLAUDE.md Router Template

```markdown
# CLAUDE.md

## About Me
[2-3 sentences. Name, what you do, who you serve.]

## Brand Files
- `./brand-files/brand.md` — My voice, tone, style, banned language
- `./brand-files/offer.md` — My offer, pricing, transformation
- `./brand-files/icp.md` — My ideal client, pains, their language

## Key Rules
- Always read brand.md before writing content
- Match my voice — [1 key characteristic]
- Speak to my audience using their language from icp.md
```

---

**How to use:**
1. Copy the template for each file
2. Replace the `[brackets]` with your actual information
3. Save each file
4. Update your CLAUDE.md to point to them
