## Content Creation

**1. Weekly Content Plan**
"Plan my content for this week. 5 posts — mix of formats. Each one should speak to my audience's specific pain points and use my voice."

**2. Hook Generator**
"Write 10 Instagram hooks about [topic]. Make them sound like me, not like a marketing template. Reference the language my audience actually uses."

**3. Carousel Outline**
"Create a 7-slide carousel about [topic]. Opening hook, 5 value slides, CTA. Write the copy in my voice — short sentences, no fluff."

**4. Email Newsletter**
"Write this week's email newsletter about [topic]. Use my tone. Address my audience's real frustrations. End with a soft CTA."

**5. LinkedIn Post**
"Write a LinkedIn post about [lesson I learned this week]. Personal story format. My voice. Something my ideal client would screenshot."

## Client Communication

**6. Welcome Message**
"Write a welcome message for a new client who just signed up. Warm, professional, sets expectations. Sounds like me, not a template."

**7. Check-In Template**
"Create a weekly client check-in message. Ask about progress, celebrate wins, identify blockers. My tone."

**8. Testimonial Request**
"Write a message asking [client name] for a testimonial. Not awkward. Natural. Reference the specific results they've gotten."

**9. Follow-Up Sequence**
"Write a 3-message follow-up sequence for someone who expressed interest but hasn't booked. Not pushy. Genuine. My voice."

**10. Referral Ask**
"Write a referral request to send to [client name]. Reference their results. Make it easy for them to forward."

## Strategy & Research

**11. Audience Deep Dive**
"Research what my ideal clients are complaining about on Reddit and forums right now. Find exact quotes and phrases they use."

**12. Competitor Gap Analysis**
"Find 5 competitors in my niche. What are they doing well? Where are the gaps I could fill? Be specific."

**13. Content Audit**
"Look at my last 10 posts [paste links or descriptions]. What's working? What patterns do you see? What should I do more of?"

**14. Positioning Statement**
"Write 3 different positioning statements for my business. Each one should differentiate me from competitors in my space."

**15. Offer Refinement**
"Here's my current offer: [describe]. How would you refine the messaging to better speak to my audience's desires and objections?"

## Operations

**16. SOP Creator**
"Create an SOP for [process I do regularly]. Step-by-step. Clear enough that someone new could follow it."

**17. Workshop Outline**
"Create a 60-minute workshop outline on [topic]. Engaging, interactive, ends with a clear next step. My teaching style."

**18. Onboarding Doc**
"Write a client onboarding document. What they need to know, what to expect, first steps. Professional but warm."

**19. Weekly Planning**
"Help me plan my week. I need to [list priorities]. Sequence these in order of impact. Block time for deep work."

**20. Quarterly Review**
"Help me review the last quarter. What did I ship? What worked? What didn't? What should I focus on next quarter?"
