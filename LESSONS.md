## Lesson 1: Your AI's Brain

Type `/cos-course:lesson-1`

I'm going to ask you 5 questions about you and your business. Then Claude creates a **CLAUDE.md file** — a cheat sheet it reads every single time you talk to it. No more generic responses. From this point on, everything is personalized to YOUR business, YOUR clients, YOUR voice.

Then I prove it works. You pick a task — content ideas, client outreach, or a welcome email — and watch Claude nail it with your specific business details baked in.

**What you'll walk away with:**
- A personalized AI assistant that knows your business
- Proof that it actually works (not just theory)

**Gift: Claude Code Cheat Sheet** — modes, shortcuts, commands, concepts. Everything you need on one page.

**Steps:**
1. Open your terminal, then type `claude`
2. Then type `/cos-course:lesson-1` and hit enter
3. Answer 5 questions about your business (just talk like you're talking to a friend)
4. Approve the pop-up when Claude creates your CLAUDE.md file
5. Watch the personalization in action

When you're done, type `/cos-course:lesson-2` to continue.

## Lesson 3: The Architecture Upgrade

Type `/cos-course:lesson-3`

Your CLAUDE.md works. But right now everything is jammed into one file. Your brand voice is different from your offer details. Your offer is different from your ideal client's pain points.

We split your context into **3 brand files** (brand.md, offer.md, icp.md), slim your CLAUDE.md into a router, and watch TWO quality jumps — one from better architecture, one from a research agent that finds your audience's REAL language from Reddit and forums.

**What you'll walk away with:**
- 3 brand files — voice, offer, audience
- A slimmed CLAUDE.md that works as a router (under 200 words)
- An upgraded carousel-copy skill that reads all brand files
- Real audience research enriching your icp.md
- Two visible quality jumps in your carousel output

**Gift: Brand File Templates** — blank templates for brand.md, offer.md, icp.md. Fill them in for any business.

**Steps:**
1. Type `/cos-course:lesson-3` and hit enter
2. Learn why multi-file architecture matters
3. Answer quick interview questions to build brand.md, offer.md, and icp.md
4. Watch your CLAUDE.md get slimmed to a router
5. Run `/carousel-copy` and see the first quality jump
6. Watch Claude research your audience's real language live
7. Run `/carousel-copy` again and see the second quality jump

## Lesson 5: Your AI Team — The Carousel Agent

Type `/cos-course:lesson-5`

You have 2 skills now. `/carousel-copy` for slides. `/caption-writer` for captions. Two commands, two outputs.

What if you could type ONE thing and get both? 10 slides + a caption. One ask, one complete package. That's not a skill — that's an **agent**. Skills are commands YOU type. Agents are team members Claude delegates to.

**What you'll walk away with:**
- Understanding the difference between skills and agents
- A carousel-writer agent that produces slides + caption from one ask
- A complete carousel content package from a single command

**Gift: Agent Workflow Templates** — 5 multi-step agent patterns (content blitz, market research, client engagement, workshop prep, weekly brief).

**Steps:**
1. Type `/cos-course:lesson-5` and hit enter
2. Learn skills vs agents (with the solopreneur analogy)
3. Walk through the agent file — frontmatter, instructions, tools, rules
4. Build the carousel-writer agent
5. Ask Claude to use it — "Create a carousel about [your topic]"
6. Get the complete package — 10 slides + caption, one ask

When you're done, type `/cos-course:lesson-6` to continue.

## Lesson 6: Connect to Canva — MCP + Finale

Type `/cos-course:lesson-6`

Your carousel is text. Beautiful, personalized text — but still text. What if Claude could turn that text into **real carousel images** you can download and post to Instagram?

That's **MCP** (Model Context Protocol) — phone lines that let Claude talk to your real tools. Canva, Notion, Cal.com, Kit, Stripe. One conversation, all your tools.

We connect Canva, generate real designed slides, and export to Claude Projects so your system works on any device.

**What you'll walk away with:**
- Canva MCP connected — text becomes real carousel images
- A complete pipeline: topic → slides → caption → designed images
- Claude Projects export — your system works on web and mobile
- The full vision of what's possible

**Gifts: MCP Tools Guide** (setup instructions for Canva, Notion, Cal.com, Kit, Stripe, n8n) + **Business-in-a-Box Prompt Pack** (10 prompts, 10 complete deliverables).

**Steps:**
1. Type `/cos-course:lesson-6` and hit enter
2. Learn what MCP is (with the pipeline analogy)
3. Connect your Canva account (or skip and set up later)
4. Watch text turn into real carousel images
5. Export your files to Claude Projects for any-device access
6. See the full project arc — everything you built, lesson by lesson
